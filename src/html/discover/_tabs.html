  • Commercial Office Revamp

    Revitalizing a corporate workspace to foster creativity, collaboration, and productivity. Our team reimagined the office layout to maximize functionality and comfort, incorporating ergonomic furniture, soundproofing elements, and vibrant accent colors to energize the environment. By integrating biophilic design principles and incorporating natural elements, such as living walls and timber finishes, we cultivated a harmonious balance between work and well-being.

  • Cozy Cafe Conceptualization

    Crafting a cozy and welcoming ambiance for a new cafe concept. From conceptualization to execution, we curated a warm and inviting atmosphere that encourages patrons to linger and savor every moment. Soft textiles, warm lighting, and rustic accents were meticulously selected to evoke a sense of comfort and nostalgia, while strategic space planning optimized seating arrangements for socializing or solitude.

  • Residential Apartment Remodel

    Redefining urban living through a thoughtful apartment remodel. Our team reimagined the layout of a compact urban dwelling to maximize space efficiency and functionality without compromising on style. Smart storage solutions, multifunctional furniture, and clever design tricks were employed to create a seamless flow between rooms and enhance the sense of openness. A neutral color palette with pops of vibrant hues injected personality and visual interest into the space, making it both practical and aesthetically pleasing for modern city living.
